💡 Key Insights

  • → Crossover threshold: Around σ=0.5, RL begins to outperform BO
  • → BO dominates clean environments: Superior sample efficiency when noise is low
  • → RL dominates noisy environments: Robustness through exploration and policy learning
  • → Curiosity module is critical: PPO+ICM significantly beats PPO baseline at high noise
  • → Real-world applicability: Most physical R&D has σ > 0.5 (measurement error, environmental variation)
